Ladybugs

Interesting Facts

  • North America has more different species of ladybugs than anywhere else in the world.

  • Although there are 23 different species of the common ladybugs in Newfoundland, they are considered a "vulnerable" insect in Labrador.

  • Most ladybugs are female. Approximately 80-90% of all eggs laid are female.

  • If you find a ladybug inside your house or a building, it's likely seeking shelter. However, they can't stay inside for long periods as they may die from dehydration and starvation. Always carry them outside to help them find their way back to nature.

  • Many countries have their own beliefs regarding the meanings or signs behind having a ladybug landing on them. Some of the meanings are associated with vitality, love, prosperity and good luck.

  • Farmers consider an abundent of ladybugs of ladybugs flying around that they will have a great harvest. Yellow ladybugs symbolizes you are going to travel soon. Many cultures consider that ladybugs symbolize that there will be a new baby born soon.
